3. Data about V.N. Veniaminov

Victor Nikolaevich Veniaminov
(The 13th of April 1935 y. – the 15th of March 2005 y.) –
The Grand doctor of philosophy, cand.tech.sci.,
the member of Presidium of “The International
Higher education academy of sciences” (“IHEAS”),
academician of “The International
academy of information”,
the deputy chairman of “The Council
of association of non-state High schools of Russia”,
The President of “The association of non-state
High schools N.-W. FD of The Russian Federation,
the member of Presidiums of “The Councils
of rectors of High schools
of The Saint-Petersburg and North-west federal district”,
the member-correspondent of “RANS”,
the professor of “The International University of quality”,
“The honourable consul of The Great duchy Luxembourg
in Saint-Petersburg city and Great Novgorod city”;

in 1959 y. has ended “Military engineering
academy of connection”
on spec. “Control systems of connection”,
worked in GDR;

in 1966 y. worked in “Military engineering
academy of connection”
in quality of the head of laboratory,
and then the teacher
on subfaculty of “Electronic elements
of military technics of connection”;

in 1974 y. has finished the candidate dissertation (thesis);
 

in 1975 y. at the organization of “The Leningrad
Supreme military-engineering school of connection”
the deputy head of subfaculty of “Electronic elements
of military technics of connection” is appointed,
subsequently the deputy head of Schools
(pro-rector) on educational and scientific work
(together with him other teachers went in school
from “Military-engineering academy of connection”
which were creators and masterpiece-makers
of the new educational organizations
becoming one the best military
High school of the country also;
pride of school was created the faculty
of “Automated control systems”);

Victor Nikolaevich Veniaminov has developed
and conducted disciplines (rates)
“Electronic elements of technics and connection”
and “Integrated electronic elements”;

he was the co-author of textbook for High school
of connection “Micro-schemes and their application”;

in 1989 y. has received
the scientific degree of the professor;

in 1990-93 y. on promotion of cadets of school
has been elected by deputy of “Lensovet” and soon –
the member of The presidium of “The Leningrad city
Council of People's Deputies”;
worked in the commission
of “The Committee on science and Higher school”,
accepted active participation
in development of the conception of evolution
of science and High school in region;
work in “Lensovet” was turning
point in his life;

the political and economic conditions in the country
demanded new forms in the educational system,
and Victor Nikolaevich Veniaminov has accepted
the offer of young initiative deputies
K.V. Smirnov and J.D. Derevjanko
to create and head one of the first in the country
non-state High schools –
“The International banking institute”
(«Международный банковский институт»);

in 1991 y. has created one of the first
non-state High school of Russia –
“The IBI” and became its rector
(under management V.N. Veniaminov “The IBI”
has occupied II place in the “Rating
of The Ministry of Education of RF”
among non-state High schools of Russia – 2002 y.,
became the winner of “The competition
of Saint-Petersburg on quality” – 2003 y.,
“The competition of The Ministry
of Education of RF – 2003 y.,
“The International competition on quality
in Monte Carlo” – 2003 y.);

“The IBI” – one of the first non-state High school,
which has received The State license
of The Ministry of Education of RF,
it is accepted in collective members of “IHEAS”,
is the unique representative of Russia
in the most prestigious community
of bank institutes of The World –
“The European network of bank training”;

in 1997 y. he is appointed by “The Honourable consul
of The Great Duchy Luxembourg
in Saint Petersburg city and Great Novgorod city”,
was the member of The Presidium
of “The public council of Saint Petersburg”,
was the member of “The World club of petersburgers
(city dwellers of Saint-Petersburg)”;

he is awarded with “The diplomatic Silver medal
named after A.M. Gorchakov”;

in 2001 y. has been awarded with
“The Award of Friendship”;

the expert in the field of management
and control systems in technics and economics,
the organizer of professional training
in system of the Higher school,
the author more than 200 scientific works
in the field of management,
quality management in education,
control systems and connection, microelectronics,
pedagogics and Higher school.
